Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties does SSS Investment Group acquire?

We focus on office and professional buildings serving service-oriented tenants such as medical, wellness, and professional services.

What markets do you target?

Our primary focus is Dallas–Fort Worth, with plans to expand across select Texas markets.

Do you work with brokers?

Yes. We actively engage with brokers and welcome off-market and marketed opportunities.

What is your investment horizon?

We pursue long-term ownership, prioritizing steady income and appreciation over short-term exits.

How do you manage risk?

Through conservative leverage, diversified tenant mix, fixed-rate debt, and planned capital reserves.
